About Sangeet Yatra

Welcome to Sangeet Yatra, a spectacular two-day music fest organized by the vibrant clubs of Manipal University Jaipur, celebrated during the auspicious holy festival of Jagannath Yatra. This grand event promises to be an unforgettable experience, filled with mesmerizing performances and a vibrant atmosphere.

OUR VISION

Sangeet Yatra aims to bring together music enthusiasts from all walks of life to celebrate the joy of music and the spirit of festivity. We believe in creating a platform where talent meets passion, and where the audience can immerse themselves in the magic of live performances.
